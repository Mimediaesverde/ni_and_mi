EQUALITY LOANS

The gender pay gap between men and women is still a latent problem in the world. It is estimated that it would take 105 years to bridge that gap.

Bankinter wanted to help reduce that time. This loan that you can pay back in 105 years, aimed at entrepreneurial women and businesses that commit to provide a fair salary among their workers. That way, they'll have no excuse.

We'll host a landing page where all the necessary information regarding how to access and manage loans will be available, as well as a pay gap meter which we hope will reach zero soon.

EQUALITY TIME

In Peru, "La hora Inca Kola" is famous for telling the time between radio commercials.

In a country where cases of femicide are worsening over the years, it was time to make the problem visible in a clearer and more direct way. The hour of equality was a tool to keep telling the time while showing real data about the situation in Peru.

SNIPER TEST

Taking care of our eyes means wearing the appropriate glasses, but many young people refuse to get their eyes tested because they are too lazy to do it or because they think their eyes are just fine, until it is too late.

How can Multiópticas, an eyewear store, make young people get their eyes tested? Playing of course.

We'll create a new firearm, a sniper rifle, with a scope with variable magnification, and we'll link it to some of the most popular shooters like Call of Duty, Fortnite and Counter-Strike. When you download this rifle, you'll try it out in a tutorial with small targets and short missions. When the tutorial is over, you'll see your score and your eye test results, showing you if you might need to visit the eye doctor.
